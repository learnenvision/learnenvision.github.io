BEHAVIORISM

Behaviorism is a learning theory that only focuses on objectively observable behaviors and discounts any independent activities of the mind. Behavior theorists define learning as nothing more than the acquisition of new behavior based on environmental conditions.This theory is relatively simple to understand because it relies only on observable behavior and describes several universal laws of behavior.
